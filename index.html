Valve Corporation ([vælv], also known as Valve Software, from English — "valve, valve") is an American private company engaged in the development, publication and digital distribution of computer games. Its most famous products are the digital distribution service of games and software Steam, computer games and series Half-Life, Portal, Counter-Strike, Left 4 Dead and Dota 2. Most of the games developed by Valve use its own game engines Source and Source 2. Headquarters - the company's apartment is located in Bellevue, Washington, not far from Seattle[3].
